Pancake Recipe

Description

  • Pancakes, are also known as a hotcake, flapjack or a griddlecake. It's a flat type of cooked batter bread. It's thin and round, and it's made from starch based batter that's then cooked on a hot surface. Pancakes are one of the most delish breakfast menu items that taste like home!

    • Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 cup Flour
    • 2 tablespoons Sugar
    • 1 tablespoon Baking Powder
    • 1 1/4 cup Milk
    • 1-2 Eggs
    • 2 tablespoons Butter
    • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract

    Steps

    1. Turn on stove ahead of time to a low/medium heat
    2. You are going to first mix in the flour, sugar, baking powder. These are all the dry ingredients.
    3. Next, You will add in your wet ingredients such as milk, vanilla extract, butter, and an egg or two, depending on how many your making.
    4. You are going to stir all of these ingredients in together until you get a nice bubbly batter like texture. Make sure it's not too thin or thick.
    5. Then, you will add in your butter to coat the pan, this way the pancake won't burn too easily. You will then pour about a 1/4 cup of your batter into your warmed up skillet pan
    6. Next, you will let your pancake rest in the pan, until the underside is lightly golden brown. At this point you are going to want to flip over your pancake.
    7. Make sure to only cook your pancakes for 1-4 minutes, to make sure they aren't browning too quickly.
    8. Once that side is brown, you can take your pancake out of the skillet and onto your plate.
    9. Follow the steps again, if you need to make more pancakes, based on the serving side you need.
    10. Afterwards, load your pancake up with some butter and maple syrup and boom! There you go a perfect pancake breakfast.
