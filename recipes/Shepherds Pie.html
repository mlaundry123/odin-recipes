Shepherd's Pie Recipe

Description

  • It's a nice baked and savory dish mixed with meat, mashed potatoes and corn.This meal originally came from the United Kingdom. It's a great meal that you can really make your own. You can add more veggies or switch up the meats. Whatever your preferences, this meal will still taste good!

    • Ingredients

    • Mashed Potatoes
    • Beef
    • onions
    • Corn
    • Paprika
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Garlic
    • Butter

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 Fahrenheit.
    2. First Your going to pre-chop your garlic and onions.
    3. After, grab your potatoes and peel and chop them up
    4. Next, Grab your skillet sandpit it on the oven to about a medium burn. You are going to put in your garlic and onions and saute them with some butter or olive oil
    5. After, You will add in your meat of choice and add in some salt and pepper and whatever type of other flavoring you would like.
    6. Then, stir the onions, meat and seasonings in and make sure everything comes together and the meat is browned..
    7. Then at this point, you can add in your corn and whatever vegetables you would like. And let it simmer a bit.
    8. At this point you can boil your potatoes until they are soft. Once they are soft you can drain the potatoes.
    9. At this point, you should add butter, salt, pepper and your other seasonings to the potatoes and start mashing until they are smooth.
    10. Then you will take your casserole dish and put in the meat and corn mixture, and top it with your mashes potatoes.
    11. You are going to bake this casserole for maybe 20-30 minutes. At least until the casserole is a little golden browned.
