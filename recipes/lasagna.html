Lasagna Recipe

Description

  • Lasagna is an Italian classic homemade dish. It's stacked alternating layers of pasta, pasta sauce, cheeses, sometimes veggies, seasonings and spices. This dish has been around for ages, but yet, it never gets old!

    • Ingredients

    • Ricotta cheese mixture
    • Meat Sauce
    • Mozzarella Cheese
    • Long Lasagna Noodles

    Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 375 Fahrenheit.
    2. First spoon in the meat sauce on the bottom of a casserole dish
    3. Next, Add in the lasagna noodles that are freshly boiled
    4. After, Put on the ricotta cheese and spread evenly on top of the noodles
    5. Then, you will then repeat the 1, 2, 3 steps about two more times. Or, at least until you reach the top of your casserole dish.
    6. Then you will take your mozzarella cheese and sprinkle it on top.
    7. Put the casserole dish into your oven that's already reheated for about 30-45 minutes.
    8. Once the casserole is all done cooking, take it out and let it rest for about 15 minutes or so.
